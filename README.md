# McDonald's South America Sales Dashboard (2021–2022)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes McDonald's sales performance across South America using an Excel dashboard. The dashboard highlights key business metrics such as sales performance, profit, customer growth, monthly trends, country-wise revenue, and customer satisfaction.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate how data visualization can help identify business insights and support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Visualization
- Business Analysis

---

## Key KPIs

**Sales**
- Total Sales: $2,544M  
- Target: $3,000M  
- Achievement: 85%

**Profit**
- Total Profit: $890M  
- Target: $1,000M  
- Achievement: 89%

**Customers**
- Total Customers: 87M  
- Target: 100M  
- Achievement: 87%

The company is performing well but still below its overall targets.

---

## Sales Trend Analysis
The dashboard compares monthly sales between 2021 and 2022.

Sales increased during the early months of the year, dropped during the middle months, and then recovered strongly toward the end of the year. The highest sales were recorded in December.

This trend suggests that seasonal demand and promotional activities influence revenue performance.

---

## Country Performance
Sales across South America show that Argentina generates the highest revenue in the region. Brazil and Ecuador also perform strongly, while Chile and Bolivia contribute comparatively lower sales.

This indicates that some markets have higher growth potential and may benefit from targeted marketing strategies.

---

## Customer Satisfaction Insights
Customer satisfaction was measured using five service factors:

- Speed
- Quality
- Hygiene
- Service
- Availability

The analysis shows strong performance in product availability, hygiene, and food quality. However, service speed and customer service scores are relatively low and require improvement.

Improving operational efficiency can significantly enhance customer experience.

---

## Key Business Insights
- Sales reached 85% of the target.
- Argentina is the top-performing country.
- Sales increase significantly in the final quarter of the year.
- Product quality and hygiene receive high satisfaction scores.
- Service speed and service quality need improvement.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Improve service speed by optimizing staff allocation and order processing.
- Focus marketing campaigns on lower-performing countries.
- Replicate successful strategies used in top-performing markets.
- Introduce loyalty programs and promotional offers to attract more customers.
